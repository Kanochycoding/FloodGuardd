General Tips During Flooding

  • Move to higher ground immediately when water levels start rising.
  • Do not drive or walk through moving floodwater.
  • Switch off electricity in flooded areas if it is safe to do so.
  • Keep emergency numbers and a charged phone ready.
  • Use only safe drinking water and avoid contaminated floodwater.
  • Follow updates from NADMO, local authorities, and trusted news alerts.

Flood Guard Safety Tips

  • Do not cross flooded roads or streams.
  • Keep NADMO and local district emergency contacts ready.
  • Move valuables and documents to elevated safe areas.
